TrabahoLens — Philippine Job Market Visualizer

This is a research tool that visualizes Philippine occupations using curated labor-market fields and PSA-aligned categories. Each rectangle's area is proportional to total employment. Color shows the selected metric — toggle between AI exposure, avg. wage, education, hiring intensity, OFW share, and informal share.

LLM-powered coloring: This adaptation of karpathy.ai/jobs includes a pipeline for writing custom prompts to score and color occupations. The AI exposure layer is one example and can be replaced with any rubric you want.

Caveat: AI Exposure measures generative AI and LLM-driven task reshaping risk. It does NOT include machinery automation (AVs, robots) or platform algorithms (dispatch, routing). These are real disruption sources but are distinct from LLM exposure.

Data: PSA LFS & OWS 2024 · PSA Survey on Overseas Filipinos 2024 · O*NET 30.2 (US DOL, CC BY 4.0) · PhilJobNet · IBPAP · POEA
